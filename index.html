Track your buses

With ChapaTuBus, you can easily track the location of your buses in real-time. Stay informed about the current whereabouts of your buses and plan your journey more efficiently.

Monitor your buses

ChapaTuBus allows you to monitor the status and performance of your buses. Keep an eye on important metrics such as speed, route adherence, and passenger capacity to ensure smooth operations and timely arrivals.
